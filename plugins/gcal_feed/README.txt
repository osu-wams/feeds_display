This module provide a Live Feeds parser for Google Calendars.
Live Feeds is used on OSU Drupal sites to display feeds from various sources.
This module will allow people to embed a feed from their Google calendar on a Drupal site.
The Google calendar must be public and you must have the full url to the feed.
Ex: http://www.google.com/calendar/feeds/liebermp%40onid.oregonstate.edu/public/full
